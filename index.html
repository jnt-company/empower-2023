Empower 2023 Interns

Welcome to JNT Empower

Empower is JNT Company's unique internship program designed for students to gain real experience in the marketing world. The program is sponsored by local businesses in Manhattan & surrounding areas, thereby giving interns the opportunity to network with business owners and learn from their expertise.

JNT invites both marketing and computer science students to join Empower. Our agency utilizes skills from multiple disciplines and gives interns the chance to expand their knowledge in their desired field.

Marketing interns have the opportunity to create a website from scratch, learn about brand strategy & videography, plan events, manage social media, and more. Computer science interns have the opportunity to work on a variety of web development and application projects, shadow our front-end and back-end developers, and learn different coding languages and tools.

All interns participate in Lunch & Learns with the program's sponsors, participate in fun group events, and learn from JNT team members about working for an agency, and different areas of marketing and development.

Whether it's marketing, web development, PR, graphic design, content writing, web or video production, there's a place for you in Empower and we want you to bring your passions to our program.
